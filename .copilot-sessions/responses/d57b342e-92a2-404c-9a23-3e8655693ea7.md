## What was wrong
The `1D`/`1W`/etc. buttons were **trailing-window filters in days** ([App.tsx](web/src/App.tsx)). Since the source data is **daily** candles, `1D` kept only the candles from the last 1 day (≈1 candle) and `1W` ≈5. With one or two candles there's nothing to pan and the time axis has nothing to label — hence "no multiple candles, no time at the bottom, can't drag."

## What I changed
1. [web/src/App.tsx](web/src/App.tsx): replaced the day-window `filterTimeframe` with `resampleCandles`, so the buttons now select a **candle interval** and aggregate the full history into that bar size:
   - `1D`/`ALL` → raw daily bars
   - `1W` → weekly bars
   - `1M` → monthly, `3M` → quarterly, `1Y` → yearly
   
   Each bucket uses first-open, last-close, max-high, min-low, summed-volume. Default is now `1D` (shows all daily candles). This guarantees many candles at every timeframe.

2. [web/src/components/CandleChart.tsx](web/src/components/CandleChart.tsx): made the time scale explicit and visible (`timeVisible`, `secondsVisible: false`, `rightOffset`, `barSpacing`) and explicitly enabled `handleScroll`/`handleScale` so the bottom time axis renders and the chart pans/zooms by drag.

Now selecting `1W`, `1M`, etc. shows weekly/monthly candles across the whole history with a draggable time axis.
